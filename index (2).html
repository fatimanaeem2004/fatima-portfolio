About Me

Storytelling first. Strategy right behind it.

I fell into content creation almost by accident — filming little clips of food and everyday Karachi life just because I liked how they looked.

Two and a half years and 85+ pieces of content later, it's become a full practice. I shoot, edit, and publish everything myself — chasing the hook that stops the scroll, the pacing that keeps someone watching, the one frame worth screenshotting. Every reel is a small design problem, and I like solving it.

Somewhere along the way, brands started noticing. What began as a personal page turned into independent work with 20+ brands, on-ground coverage at real events, and an account, @fn.cameraroll_, that reaches half a million people a month without a single rupee spent on ads.

These days I think about content the way a social media manager does — not just "does this look good," but "does this actually grow the page." That's the lens I bring to every collaboration.
